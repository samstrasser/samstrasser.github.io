Startup Advising

You should consider working with me if

  1. You want your product and engineering teams to build faster

    Do you miss the good ol' days when, all in a single day, you talked to a customer, had an idea, told someone, got it built, and then that customer and hundreds of others told you they loved it? Do you wonder why, now that you're spending 10x (or 1,000x) more on EPD, you just can't seem to build like that anymore? Lots of people will tell you that it's inevitable as you scale and there is no way around it, but I disagree. I've been there many times and I've helped many companies out of that pit. It's not as hard as people make it out to be!

  2. You need help figuring out EPD hiring

    Do you feel like you're supposed to hire a VP of Engineering but you're not really sure why, or what kind, or when? Do you waffle between wanting to elevate your founding engineer and doubting whether they're a good fit to lead the team? Do you wonder whether you really need all these engineers, especially given LLMs?

  3. You want stability through a transition in EPD leadership

    Do you need to replace your CTO / VPE / CPO / Head of Product, but you are hesitant because you know backfilling will be tough? Or perhaps they've told you they'd like to leave and you need to figure out a short-term plan? Do you want to delay thinking about an engineering or product leader for a little while as you focus on other priorities in the business?

What I Do (A Menu)

  • Manage or coach your senior-most engineering or product leaders
  • Join your exec team to represent product, engineering or both
  • Design hiring loops to find the right-fit VP / Director of Engineering / Product / Data
  • ...and interview candidates for those roles (usually in the later rounds)
  • Set the product roadmap, aligned to company strategy
  • Identify high-upside product bets and help you decide which ones are worth making
  • Define your product KPIs and other product metrics
  • Run your weekly planning or other product ceremonies
  • Stabilize your platform if it is not reliable or struggling as you've scaled
  • Establish or improve collaboration with sales, marketing, customer success, ops and other cross-functional teams
  • Engage with your board on technical strategy and tech organization strategy and planning

Not seeing something you'd like me to do? Just ask! Think of me as an EPD jack-of-all-trades.

I work in three phases:

  1. Diagnose (and stabilize, if necessary)
  2. Decide what to tackle, and then tackle it
  3. Downshift (over time)
